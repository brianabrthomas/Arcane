# Arcane · Judging Demo Script

Welcome to Arcane! This script is designed to help hackathon judges verify the integrations for the **Circle**, **Arc**, and **Ledger** bounties. 

You can perform this demo on the live URL provided in our submission, or by running the app locally.

---

## 🛑 Before You Start
If you are testing on the live URL, simply open the page.
If you are running locally, ensure your `.env` has `PAYMENTS_LIVE=true` and a valid `ARC_OPERATOR_PRIVATE_KEY` funded with Arc Testnet USDC.

---

## 🎬 Step 1: Market Creation & UI Overview (Arc Bounty)
1. Open the Arcane web app.
2. Look at the **Market Feed** on the left. These are real patent litigation cases pulled from the CourtListener API.
3. Click on the first market: *"Will Amarin win the SCOTUS skinny-label inducement ruling?"*
4. Look at the **Settlement Contract** panel on the right sidebar. 
5. Notice the **Status: OPEN**.
6. Click the **[ArcScan]** link next to the Contract Address. 
   * **Verification:** This proves the `ArcaneSettlement.sol` contract is live on the Arc Testnet.

---

## 🎬 Step 2: EIP-712 Trading & Arc Settlement (Arc Bounty)
1. In the top right, click **Connect Wallet** and select **Demo Wallet**. (This bypasses MetaMask popups for faster testing, but still generates valid EIP-712 typed data under the hood).
2. In the Trading Interface, select **YES**.
3. Enter an amount under $100 (e.g., **$50**).
4. Click **Execute Trade**.
5. Look at the **Settlement Contract** panel again. Under the "Recent Trades" section, a new row will appear.
6. Click the **[ArcScan]** link next to your trade.
   * **Verification:** You will see the `buy()` transaction confirmed on Arc Testnet, showing the USDC escrow transfer and the `TradeExecuted` event.

---

## 🎬 Step 3: Ledger Hardware Policy (Ledger Bounty)
1. Now, try to execute a high-risk trade. Enter an amount over $100 (e.g., **$150**).
2. Click **Execute Trade**.
3. **Verification:** The trade will be blocked by the backend policy engine. A modal will appear stating: *"Ledger hardware device approval required (>= $100 USDC)."*
4. Look at the code snippet displayed in the modal. This is the exact JavaScript generated by our backend using the **Ledger DMK**, showing the EIP-712 clear-signing fields (`Market`, `Side`, `Amount`, `Max Price`) that would appear on the physical device screen.

---

## 🎬 Step 4: Agentic Pipeline & x402 Nanopayments (Circle Bounty)
1. Scroll down to the **Agentic Research** panel.
2. Click the **Run Agent Analysis** button.
3. Watch the live terminal feed. You will see 9 different AI agents spinning up.
4. Look at the **x402 Payment Status** panel on the right sidebar.
5. **Verification:** As the agents work, you will see real-time USDC nanopayments being logged. For example, `Orchestrator` pays `CaseScout` $0.005 USDC to fetch docket PDFs, and pays `ProbabilityAgent` $0.01 USDC to run the final LMSR forecast.
6. This demonstrates a fully functional machine-to-machine economy using the Circle Agent Stack concepts.

---

## 🎬 Step 5: Resolution & Dispute Lifecycle (Arc Bounty)
*(Note: To prevent users from resolving live markets, this step requires Admin privileges. If you are on the live demo URL, the backend is configured to allow demo resolutions for judging purposes).*

1. In the **Settlement Contract** panel, locate the **Admin Actions** section.
2. Select **YES** as the outcome and click **Propose Resolution**.
3. **Verification:** The contract status will instantly change to **PROPOSED**. 
4. Notice the **Dispute Window Ends At** timestamp. The contract strictly locks payouts for 24 hours to allow community disputes.
5. Click the **[ArcScan]** link to verify the `proposeResolution()` transaction on-chain.
6. Because this is a demo environment, we have enabled a bypass to finalize immediately. Click **Finalize**.
7. The status changes to **FINALIZED**.
8. Finally, click **Claim Payout**. The contract will calculate your share of the winning pool and transfer the USDC to your wallet.
